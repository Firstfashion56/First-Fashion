Nike Jordan

Nike Jordan, or Air Jordan, is a premium footwear and apparel line created by Nike in collaboration with basketball legend Michael Jordan. Launched in 1985, it revolutionized sports fashion with the iconic Air Jordan 1, which was banned by the NBA for its colors but gained fame through Jordan's defiance. The brand expanded beyond shoes into clothing and accessories, becoming a global symbol of athletic excellence. Known for its "Jumpman" logo and style, Air Jordans continue to influence not just basketball, but fashion and urban culture worldwide.
